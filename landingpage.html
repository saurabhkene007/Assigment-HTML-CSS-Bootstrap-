Mission

  • To improve the quality of education in rural area and to help students in making right career choices and decisions.
  • To guide and train the students toward achieving excellence in their professional and personal lives.
  • To provide financial assistance to creditable students who cannot afford their higher education.
  • To support girls for getting higher education and empower women through education.
  • To drive collective action to accelerate positive social change.



Aadhaar Global Foundation

Our Vision

Aadhaar envisions a world in which all young people able to determine their full potential to create positive social change.

Welcome

Aadhaar Global Foundation

Founded in the year 2017 Aadhaar Global Foundation tries to break vicious cycle of poverty by helping underprivileged children's to get quality education. Career guidance and planning is a very important aspect in shaping a child's future. Therefore, Aadhaar Global Foundation seeks to inspire & direct young people to achieve excellence in their professional and personal lives. Our belief is that eventually such motivated young people will bring positive change in the society.

Our Objectives

  • Create career opportunity awareness among young people through career guidance seminars in rural & semi-rural areas.
  • Conduct programs for students and unemployed rural youth.
  • Support the students to achieve academic excellence so as to accomplish better employability.
  • capability of young people to grow as responsible and independent adults.
  • the youth to support the nation building processes in and around them.
  • Counsel parents of girls to make them understand the importance of education.
  • Motivate people to participate and contribute for development of education.
